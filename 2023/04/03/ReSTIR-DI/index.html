ReSTIR DI

ReSTIR DI

This project implements the Reservoir-based Spatiotemporal Importance Resampling (ReSTIR) to render multiple-light scenes.

The ReSTIR is known for achieving real-time rendering in interactive lighting scenes without extra complex data structures or high-sample costs. Generally speaking, it combines Resampled Importance Sampling (RIS), Weighted Reservoir Sampling (WRS), and Spatio-temporal Reuse to provide those outstanding features: 1) approximate perfect importance sampling proportionally to a target PDF, which no practical sampling algorithm may exist (e.g. $\rho \cdot L_e \cdot G$); 2) using a constant length “reservoir” to store accepted samples; 3) reusing information from nearby samples both spatially and temporally.
